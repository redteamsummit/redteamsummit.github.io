Welcome to RTS 2019

Red Team Summit is an invite-only hacking conference for the red teams, by the red teams. Offensive security operators present various TTPs to share lessons learned from accumulated successes and failures.

Intended audience and participants are in-house pentest or red team staff who engage in operations against their employer's environment(s) as external attackers. We also welcome external red teamers who provide offensive engagement service to their customers.

Does your team write their own implants, run their own infra, and get shells? If so, then this conference is for you.
