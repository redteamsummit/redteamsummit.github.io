Code of Conduct

The Red Team Summit hosted at Amazon is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone. We encourage all participants to make each other feel welcome and included in activities throughout the conference. We do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form. Harassment includes, but is not limited to, deliberate intimidation and targeting individuals in a manner that makes them feel uncomfortable, unwelcome, or afraid.

Participants asked to stop any harassing behavior are expected to comply immediately. If a participant engages in harassing behavior, Amazon organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning the offender or expulsion from the event. If you are being harassed, notice that someone else is being harassed, or have any other concerns, please contact a member of Amazon staff immediately.

By participating in the Red Team Summit, whether as a speaker or participant, you agree to abide by our code of conduct.
